## Glossary

- **AMM (Automated Market Maker)**: Decentralized exchange type using liquidity pools and mathematical formulas (x*y=k) for token swaps without order books; Uniswap, PancakeSwap, Curve dominant AMM models; liquidity fragmentation across AMM variants exacerbates slippage.
- **Combinatorial Auctions**: CoW Swap feature enabling multiple trades to be batched and solved simultaneously, targeting 33% order throughput increase and improved capital efficiency through coordinated execution.
- **Cross-chain bridge**: Protocol enabling asset transfers between blockchains (Stargate, Synapse, Celer); $8B+ TVL across 43 protocols; volume $56.1B monthly peak (July 2025); avg transaction $3,489; fees $5-20; latency 30s-5min; security risk $2B+ historical hacks.
- **DEX aggregator**: Service querying multiple decentralized exchanges to find optimal swap pricing and routing (1inch, CoW Swap, Matcha, ParaSwap); single-chain aggregators achieve 1-3% price improvement vs direct DEX; cross-chain aggregation limited by latency and fees.
- **Fee tier**: Uniswap v3 feature enabling liquidity providers to choose fee percentage (0.05%/0.3%/1%); creates 3× liquidity fragmentation per token pair as pools split across tiers; reduces capital efficiency and increases slippage.
- **Impermanent loss**: Liquidity provider loss from price divergence between pooled assets vs simply holding; fragmentation reduces LP yields making impermanent loss more severe; LPs need >8% APY to justify risk, driving migration and further fragmentation.
- **Intent-based trading**: Trading model where users specify desired outcome (buy X token for <Y price) and solvers compete to execute; CoW Swap pioneered model reaching 60-90% market share of intent swaps, $12.47B Sept 2025 volume (3× monthly avg 2024).
- **L2 (Layer 2)**: Ethereum scaling solution processing transactions off-chain (Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, Polygon); each L2 has $1B-5B TVL but isolated liquidity pools; L2 migration from Ethereum ($70B+ mainnet TVL) fragments liquidity despite reducing fees.
- **Liquidity fragmentation**: Distribution of trading volume and liquidity across 100+ blockchains, 1,000+ DEX protocols, multiple fee tiers, creating inefficiencies: 2-8% slippage on mid-cap tokens (vs 0.1-0.5% optimal), 5-15% price impact on $50K+ trades, higher costs.
- **Price impact**: Unfavorable price change from large trade depleting pool liquidity; $50K+ trades in mid-cap tokens experience 5-15% price impact; fragmentation worsens price impact by splitting liquidity across multiple pools/chains.
- **Slippage**: Difference between expected and actual execution price; mid-cap tokens experience 2-8% slippage (vs 0.1-0.5% optimal for liquid pairs); long-tail tokens 10-30% slippage; fragmentation primary cause of excessive slippage.
- **Smart order routing**: Algorithm splitting trades across multiple DEXs and liquidity pools to minimize price impact and slippage; single-chain routing mature (1-3% improvement), cross-chain routing limited by latency (30s-5min) and fees ($5-20).
- **THORChain**: Cross-chain liquidity protocol enabling native asset swaps without wrapped tokens using continuous liquidity pools and validator network; <5% DEX volume market share due to complexity and limited chain support.
- **TVL (Total Value Locked)**: Dollar value of assets deposited in DeFi protocols; Ethereum $70B+ (Nov 2025), Solana $11.5B (+32.7% QoQ), L2s $10B-20B combined, cross-chain bridges $8B+; high TVL but fragmented across 100+ chains reduces capital efficiency.
- **Wrapped asset**: Tokenized representation of asset on non-native chain (WETH, bridged ETH); proliferation of wrapped variants (each bridge creates own) fragments liquidity by creating separate pools for functionally equivalent assets.
