## Glossary

- **Asset-Referenced Token (ART)**: Under EU MiCA, a token that maintains stable value by referencing multiple assets, one or more official currencies, or other values; RWA tokens backed by commodities, real estate, or art typically classified as ARTs.
- **Delaware Series LLC**: US legal structure allowing parent LLC to create segregated "series" (compartments) each holding different asset/pool with independent liability; popular for multi-asset RWA tokenization.
- **Hybrid Custody**: 2025 industry standard where off-chain custodians hold legal title to underlying assets while blockchain tracks investor token entitlements in real-time; addresses disconnect between on-chain token transfers and off-chain legal ownership.
- **MiCA (Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation)**: EU comprehensive crypto-assets regulation effective 2024-2025 creating unified framework; exempts transferable securities (which fall under MiFID II), covers asset-referenced tokens, e-money tokens, and other crypto-assets.
- **MiFID II (Markets in Financial Instruments Directive II)**: EU financial services regulation governing trading of securities; tokenized RWAs classified as "transferable securities" fall under MiFID II rather than MiCA.
- **RWA (Real-World Assets)**: Physical or traditional financial assets (real estate, commodities, art, securities, IP) represented as digital tokens on blockchain enabling fractional ownership and 24/7 trading.
- **Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV)**: Company or legal entity established to hold specific asset(s) and issue tokens representing rights against SPV; isolates asset from issuer's other liabilities but introduces bankruptcy risk if not structured as trust.
- **Title Perfection**: Legal process of establishing clear, enforceable ownership rights recognized by courts and government registries; many assets require off-chain steps (notarized deeds, registry filings) beyond on-chain token transfer.
