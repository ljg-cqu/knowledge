Formulate a structured problem statement using the following [Input] fields.

Affects DeFi protocols with $200B+ TVL requiring liquidation infrastructure, DAOs managing $20B+ treasuries facing market volatility, enterprises with $50B+ multisig assets exposed to hacks. Need to reduce emergency coordination failure rate from 25-35% to <10% and decrease response time from 3-8h to <2h by Q4 2026.
   
   - **Background and current situation**: Multi-signature security model assumes non-urgent transaction approval: signers review proposed transactions over 24-72h (geographically distributed, different time zones), verify legitimacy via due diligence, coordinate via async communication (Slack, email, governance platforms), approve at convenience [Model: Standard Multisig Workflow, 2024]. ## Glossary

- **DeFi liquidation**: Collateralized lending position (Aave, Compound, Maker) seizure when collateral value drops below threshold, <15min response window, 10-15% penalty, $200M-$400M annual multisig-related losses
- **Emergency pause mechanism**: Smart contract function enabling admin/multisig to halt protocol operations during security incidents, 60-70% major DeFi adoption, 2-6h median activation time
- **Guardian multisig**: Separate lower-threshold multisig (e.g., 2-of-3) with emergency-only powers vs higher-threshold treasury multisig (e.g., 5-of-9), reduces coordination burden but introduces centralization risk
- **Time-locked operation**: Pre-approved emergency action with 6-24h delay period (cancellable by multisig during window), enables rapid post-delay execution but insufficient for <6h emergencies
- **Pre-signed transaction**: Emergency action (pause, fund evacuation) with M signatures collected in advance and stored for rapid deployment, inflexible (cannot adapt to specific crisis), requires 30-90 day rotation
- **Incident Response (IR)**: Professional crisis management service (Cantina <15min SLA) with vulnerability surfacing, playbooks, simulations, 24/7 monitoring, $50K-$200K annual retainer
- **False alarm fatigue**: Psychological phenomenon where 95%+ non-emergency alerts reduce signer engagement and emergency drill participation to 50-60% for third+ iteration
- **Hot wallet emergency reserve**: Single-signature wallet holding $100K-$1M for rapid response (<15min), enables speed but creates vulnerability (WazirX 2024 hack), 60-70% adoption
- **Coordination failure**: Emergency scenario where multisig cannot gather M signatures within required window (25-35% complete failures), resulting in liquidations, breach escalations, market losses
- **Emergency signer psychology**: Behavioral patterns including 60-70% approval hesitation (fear of fraud), 40-50% "wait and see" preference, 20-30% stress-induced error rate, authority diffusion (bystander effect)
