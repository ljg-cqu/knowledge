## Glossary

- **Gas**: Unit measuring computational effort required to execute Ethereum transactions, priced in gwei (1 gwei = 10⁻⁹ ETH), multisig transactions consume 3-5× more gas than single-signature
- **ECDSA signature verification**: Elliptic Curve Digital Signature Algorithm recovery operation costing 3000-6000 gas per signature, M-of-N multisig requires M verifications
- **Layer-2 (L2)**: Blockchain scaling solutions (Arbitrum, Optimism, zkSync) processing transactions off Ethereum mainnet offering 10-50× gas reduction, introducing security/complexity trade-offs
- **Transaction batching**: Combining multiple operations into single multisig transaction to amortize signature verification costs across operations, achieving 30-40% cost reduction
- **Gas price (gwei)**: Ethereum transaction fee denominated in gwei (10⁻⁹ ETH), fluctuates 10-100+ gwei based on network demand, directly determines multisig transaction cost
- **Gnosis Safe**: Dominant multisig wallet platform (80%+ market share), 2-of-3 configuration consumes 150K-300K gas per transaction, highly optimized implementation
- **BLS signatures**: Boneh-Lynn-Shacham signature scheme enabling aggregation (M signatures → 1 combined signature) reducing multisig gas 60-80%, requires protocol upgrade
- **MPC (Multi-Party Computation)**: Threshold signature alternative to on-chain multisig achieving single-signature gas costs (~50K gas), different trust model from explicit multisig
- **EIP-4337 (Account Abstraction)**: Ethereum improvement enabling gas sponsorship, native transaction batching, early-stage multisig integration <10% adoption
- **Cross-chain bridge**: Protocol enabling asset transfer between Ethereum mainnet and Layer-2 networks, required for multisig migration, $2B+ historical hack losses
