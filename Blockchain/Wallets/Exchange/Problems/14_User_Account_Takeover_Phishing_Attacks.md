## Glossary

- **2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)**: Security mechanism requiring two verification factors (password + SMS/app/hardware token) to access accounts, reduces unauthorized access 70-80% when mandatory, adoption <60% voluntary vs 90-95% mandatory.
- **Behavioral Biometrics**: Passive fraud detection analyzing 500+ user behavior signals (mouse dynamics, typing patterns, navigation behavior), 90-95% accuracy, <100ms latency, transparent to users, platforms include BioCatch, BehavioSec, Plurilock.
- **Credential Stuffing**: Automated testing of leaked username/password combinations from other breaches, 1-5% success rate due to password reuse, mitigated by 2FA and password complexity requirements.
- **FIDO2/WebAuthn**: Open authentication standard for hardware tokens and biometric authentication, phishing-resistant cryptographic verification, supported by YubiKey, Google Titan, platform authenticators (TouchID, Windows Hello).
- **Hardware Token**: Physical device generating 2FA codes (YubiKey, Google Titan Key), 99% effective against phishing and SIM swap attacks, <5% adoption current due to cost ($20-$50/device) and UX friction, target >20% for high-value accounts.
- **Phishing**: Social engineering attack impersonating trusted entities to steal credentials, 60-70% of account takeover attacks, methods include fake emails/SMS, malicious websites, phone calls, primary threat vector 2025.
- **Session Hijacking**: Stealing authentication cookies to bypass 2FA entirely, mitigated by HTTPS strict transport security, short session timeouts (15-30min), device fingerprinting, requires user awareness of browser security.
- **SIM Swapping**: Hijacking phone numbers by social engineering mobile carriers, 100% SMS 2FA bypass rate, 5-10% of account takeover attacks, prevented by app-based or hardware tokens, carrier security hardening (PIN protection, port-out alerts).
- **TOTP (Time-based One-Time Password)**: Algorithm (RFC 6238) generating 30s validity 6-digit codes for authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy), more secure than SMS but vulnerable to real-time phishing proxies.
- **Withdrawal Whitelisting**: User pre-approves destination addresses with 24-48h delay for new addresses, 60-80% reduction in fraudulent withdrawals, 20-30% users disable due to friction, requires balance between security and UX.
