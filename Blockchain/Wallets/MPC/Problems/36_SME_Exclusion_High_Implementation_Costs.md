## Glossary

- **HSM (Hardware Security Module)**: Tamper-resistant physical device storing cryptographic keys, providing hardware-grade security; cloud HSMs cost $500-$3K/month, pricing beyond most SME budgets.
- **MPC (Multi-Party Computation)**: Cryptographic technique enabling distributed key shares across multiple parties/devices; threshold signatures require k-of-n shares, eliminating single point of failure but increasing cost/complexity.
- **Self-service onboarding**: Deployment process enabling customers to set up independently using guided wizards, documentation, automation vs. consultant-led implementation requiring $150-$300/hour specialist time.
- **SME (Small and Medium Enterprise)**: Businesses with 10-250 employees, annual revenue $200K-$10M, IT budgets $10K-$50K; 334M globally [Statistic: World Bank, 2024], 68% excluded from MPC adoption [Report: Intel Market Research, 2025].
- **Threshold signature (k-of-n)**: Cryptographic scheme requiring k shares from n total to authorize transactions; 2-of-3 common for SMEs (simpler than enterprise 4-of-7), preventing unauthorized transactions while maintaining disaster recovery.
