## Glossary

- **ERC-2771**: Ethereum standard for secure meta-transactions, allowing contracts to trust the original transaction signer even when relayed through intermediary.
- **ERC-4337**: Ethereum account abstraction standard enabling smart contract wallets with advanced features including gasless transactions.
- **EIP-3529**: Ethereum Improvement Proposal that removed gas refunds for SELFDESTRUCT and reduced SSTORE refunds, eliminating gas token economic viability (2021).
- **Gas**: Unit of computational work on blockchain networks, with each operation consuming specific gas amount. Transaction fee = gas consumed × gas price.
- **Gasless transaction**: Blockchain transaction where user doesn't directly pay gas fees; instead, a third party (relayer, DApp, or sponsor) covers the cost.
- **MEV (Maximal Extractable Value)**: Profit extractable by block producers or intermediaries through reordering, inserting, or censoring transactions within blocks.
- **Meta-transaction**: Transaction where user signs message off-chain with transaction intent, and relayer submits it on-chain, enabling gasless interactions.
- **Relayer (Relay service)**: Infrastructure provider that submits blockchain transactions on behalf of users, typically covering gas costs in gasless transaction architectures.
- **Transaction bundler**: Service that aggregates multiple transactions into single on-chain submission, reducing per-transaction overhead costs.
