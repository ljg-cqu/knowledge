## Glossary

- **CASP (Crypto-Asset Service Provider)**: EU MiCA term for entities providing crypto services, subject to licensing and KYC requirements
- **Custodial wallet**: Wallet where third party (exchange, provider) controls user's private keys and funds
- **FATF (Financial Action Task Force)**: International AML/CFT standard-setting body, issues recommendations for 37 member countries + 200+ jurisdictions
- **Form 1099-DA**: New IRS tax form (2025+) requiring brokers to report digital asset sales (gross proceeds 2025, cost basis 2026+ for 2026+ acquired assets)
- **MiCA (Markets in Crypto-Assets)**: EU regulation for crypto-asset markets, phased implementation 2024-2025
- **Money transmitter**: US state licensing category for entities transmitting money, 30-40 states apply to crypto services
- **Non-custodial wallet**: Wallet where user controls private keys, provider does not custody funds (e.g., MetaMask, Trust Wallet)
- **Travel Rule**: FATF Recommendation 16 requiring VASPs to share originator/beneficiary info for transfers >$1000/€1000
- **VASP (Virtual Asset Service Provider)**: FATF term for crypto service providers subject to AML/CFT regulations
