## Glossary

- **AI Chatbot Deflection Rate**: Percentage of support tickets resolved by AI without human escalation (current 60-75% for simple queries, 5-15% for complex financial distress)
- **Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)**: Regulatory process requiring additional KYC/AML verification for flagged accounts, typically 30-90 days to complete
- **False Positive Freeze**: Legitimate user account frozen by fraud detection algorithm incorrectly identifying suspicious activity (35-45% of frozen accounts)
- **Frozen Account**: User account with restricted access due to fraud detection, regulatory compliance, security incident, or margin call (100K-500K industry-wide at any time)
- **Human Escalation**: Transfer of support ticket from AI chatbot to human agent for complex issue resolution (Tier 2-4, 10-20% of tickets)
- **KYC/AML**: Know Your Customer / Anti-Money Laundering regulatory requirements mandating identity verification and transaction monitoring
- **Premium Support Tier**: Paid subscription ($30/mo) providing dedicated account manager, <6h response time, phone support (3-5% adoption, alienates mass market)
- **Self-Custody Architecture**: Wallet design where user controls private keys, provider cannot access funds (correctly designed for security but limits support capability)
- **Tier 1-4 Escalation**: Support workflow: Tier 1 (AI chatbot 80-90% tickets), Tier 2 (human generalist 3-7 day), Tier 3 (security specialist 14-30 day), Tier 4 (executive 30-90 day)
- **Trustpilot Rating**: Independent customer review platform score (1-5 stars); major wallets currently 1.3-2.8/5 ("Poor" to "Average"), industry target >4.0/5 ("Great")
- **Unresolved Ticket Backlog**: Support tickets aged >30 days without resolution (estimated 500K+ industry-wide)
