## Glossary

- **Disaster Recovery (DR)**: Business continuity planning with Recovery Point Objective (RPO) and Recovery Time Objective (RTO) metrics
- **Hardware Security Module (HSM)**: Physical device for cryptographic key storage and operations, tamper-resistant
- **Key ceremony**: Formal process for generating, distributing, or rotating cryptographic key shares in MPC systems
- **Multi-Party Computation (MPC)**: Cryptographic protocol enabling multiple parties to jointly compute functions without revealing inputs
- **RPO (Recovery Point Objective)**: Maximum acceptable data loss timeframe, measured in time before disruption
- **RTO (Recovery Time Objective)**: Maximum acceptable downtime duration for system recovery
- **Separation of duties**: Security principle requiring multiple parties to complete sensitive operations
- **SOC 2**: Service Organization Control 2 audit framework for security, availability, confidentiality
- **Threshold signature**: Cryptographic scheme requiring M-of-N key shares to authorize transaction, eliminating single point of failure
- **Vendor lock-in**: Dependency on specific vendor making migration to alternative provider difficult or costly
