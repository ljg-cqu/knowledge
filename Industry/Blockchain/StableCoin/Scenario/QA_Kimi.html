Use Case Scenarios

The following 30 evidence-based scenarios validate product management capabilities across six critical domains for blockchain stablecoin projects.

Strategy

UC1: Navigating the Stablecoin Trilemma

Advanced
15 min

Scenario: The executive team faces the "Stablecoin Trilemma" - choosing between algorithmic decentralization, fiat-collateralized centralization, or a hybrid model. With a $10M budget and 12-month deadline, the decision will irreversibly define the project's architecture and vision.

Key Capability

The product must support multi-faceted strategic decision-making by enabling comparative analysis of fundamentally different stablecoin architectures, integrating financial modeling, risk assessment, regulatory forecasting, and competitive analysis.

Validation Criteria
  • • Implements comprehensive strategic evaluation framework [Ref: L4]
  • • Multi-dimensional analysis (Product, Business, Strategic, Operational)
  • • Concrete workflow: define options → create matrix → input data → calculate scores
  • • Visualizes trade-offs for each model (Algorithmic, Fiat-Collateralized, Hybrid)
Success Metrics
  • • 90% executive alignment (post-decision survey)
  • • <4 weeks time to decision
  • • 4.5/5 quality rating (external advisor)
  • • Comprehensive risk assessment documentation
Strategic Evaluation Matrix
Criterion Weight Algorithmic Fiat-Collateralized Hybrid
Decentralization 30% 9 (High) 2 (Low) 6 (Medium)
Stability & Peg 25% 2 (High Risk) 9 (High) 5 (Medium Risk)
Capital Efficiency 20% 9 (High) 3 (Low) 6 (Medium)
Weighted Score 100% 6.3 5.1 5.7

UC2: Responding to a Depegging Crisis

Intermediate
10 min

Scenario: "StablePay USD" has depegged to $0.97 with unclear causes. The team has 24 hours to diagnose and respond before community panic escalates. Wrong moves could trigger a bank run.

Key Capability

Enable rapid, data-driven crisis management by integrating real-time market data, on-chain analytics, and communication tools to diagnose depegging events and simulate intervention strategies.

Crisis Dashboard
Real-time Metrics:
  • • Price deviation from $1.00 peg
  • • 24h volume & market cap
  • • Active addresses & whale activity
  • • Reserve health & collateralization
Response Options:
  • • Immediate public statement
  • • Deploy reserves for buybacks
  • • Wait and monitor approach
  • • Hybrid response strategies
Success Target: Restore peg within 1% band in <6 hours Framework: Crisis response playbook [Ref: A12]

UC3: Choosing a Stabilization Mechanism

Intermediate
12 min

Scenario: A product team must choose between a Liquidation-based model (like MakerDAO) or a Supply Adjustment (rebase) model for their crypto-collateralized stablecoin. Limited development resources allow only one mechanism for launch.

Evaluation Framework: Comparative analysis using weighted scoring on Peg Stability, Capital Efficiency, User Experience, Technical Complexity, and Security Risk. [Ref: L4]
