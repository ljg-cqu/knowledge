1. **Expression**: according to plan (type: collocation)
   **Meaning**: 
   - As expected or as was intended; following the predetermined schedule or strategy
   - *Fixedness*: strong (fixed phrase)
   
   **Synonyms**: as planned, as expected, as scheduled, on schedule, as intended
   
   **Antonyms**: off plan, unexpectedly, contrary to plan, not as planned
   
   **When to Use**: Project management, status updates, reporting conformity with expectations; suitable for all registers; indicates success or smooth progress
   
   **When NOT to Use**: When things deviated from plan (use "despite the plan" or "contrary to plan"); when being ironic about failure
   
   **Common Variations**: 
   - go according to plan
   - proceed according to plan
   - everything went according to plan
   - not according to plan
   
   **Why This Partnership**: 
   - *For collocations*: "Plan" is the natural object of "according to" in progress reporting contexts; conventional phrase in project management discourse
   
   **Root Analysis**: accord (agreement, harmony) + -ing + to + plan (intended course of action)
   
   **Examples [Casual]**: 
   - Everything went according to plan, so we're all set!
   - The party didn't exactly go according to plan, but it was still fun.
   
   **Examples [Formal]**: 
   - The implementation proceeded according to plan, with all milestones achieved on schedule.
   - Project execution is progressing according to plan, with no significant deviations reported.

   **Examples [Casual]**:
