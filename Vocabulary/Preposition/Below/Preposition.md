1. **Preposition**: below (type: simple)
   **Meaning**: 
   - *(Spatial)* At a lower level or position than something; underneath or beneath something without touching it
   - *(Measurement)* Less than a particular number, amount, or level
   - *(Abstract)* Lower in rank, status, or quality than something; not good enough for someone
   
   **Synonyms**: under (in context of measurement), beneath (spatial), underneath (spatial), lower than (comparison)
   
   **Antonyms**: above, over, beyond, higher than
   
   **When to Use**: When describing something at a lower position without direct contact, referring to quantities or measurements that are less than a threshold, or indicating lower rank/status in hierarchies
   
   **When NOT to Use**: When direct contact or covering is implied (use "under" instead), when referring to topics or subjects in text (use "regarding" or "concerning"), when expressing movement through something (use "under" not "below")
   
   **Common Patterns**: 
   - **Prepositional phrases**: below average, below zero, below the surface, below sea level, below the poverty line, below standard, below expectations
   - **Verb + prep**: fall below, drop below, sink below, stay below, remain below, hover below
   - **Adjective + prep**: visible below, located below, positioned below
   - **Noun + prep**: temperature below, level below, floor below, section below
   
   **Root Analysis**: be- (by, about) + low (not high)
   
   **Etymology**: Middle English bilooghe, from bi- (by) + lowe (low) â†’ developed into modern "below" meaning "at a lower position"
   
   **Examples [Casual]**: 
   - The temperature's gonna drop below freezing tonight, so watch out for ice.
   - Can you check the cupboard below the sink? I think the cleaner's in there.
   
   **Examples [Formal]**: 
   - The results indicate that performance metrics consistently remained below the established threshold throughout the quarter.
   - As illustrated in the diagram below, the correlation between variables demonstrates a significant negative trend.
