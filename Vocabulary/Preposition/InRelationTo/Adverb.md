1. **Adverb**: relatively (type: degree)
   **Meaning**: 
   - To a certain extent when compared with something else; in a way that is not absolute
   
   **Synonyms**: comparatively, somewhat, fairly, moderately
   
   **Antonyms**: absolutely, extremely, completely
   
   **When to Use**: When you want to soften a statement by framing it in relation to a standard or comparison group ("relatively small", "relatively cheap").
   
   **When NOT to Use**: When you mean something is completely or absolutely true without comparison.
   
   **Common Phrases**: 
   - relatively small/large/high/low
   - relatively speaking
   - relatively stable
   - relatively cheap/expensive

1. **Adverb**: proportionally (type: degree)

1. **Adverb**: correspondingly (type: manner)
