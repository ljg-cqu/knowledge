1. **Adjective**: compliant (comparative: more compliant, superlative: most compliant)
   **Meaning**: 
   - Meeting or agreeing with rules, standards, or requests; disposed to comply or yield
   - Used both attributively and predicatively to describe conformity with requirements
   
   **Synonyms**: conforming, obedient, cooperative, agreeable, accommodating
   
   **Antonyms**: noncompliant, defiant, resistant, uncooperative, rebellious
   
   **When to Use**: Legal, regulatory, business contexts discussing adherence to rules or standards
   
   **When NOT to Use**: Contexts where independence or innovation is valued; when describing negative submission
   
   **Common Phrases**: 
   - compliant with regulations
   - fully compliant
   - compliant systems
   - compliant behavior
   - remain compliant
   
   **Root Analysis**: comply (com- [with] + ply [fold]) + -ant (doing)
   
   **Etymology**: Italian complire → English comply (17th century) → compliant, meaning "yielding to requests"
   
   **Examples [Casual]**: 
   - Make sure your car is compliant with emissions standards.
   - He's pretty compliant with whatever the group decides.
   
   **Examples [Formal]**: 
   - All systems must be compliant with security protocols as per regulatory requirements.
   - The organization maintains compliant documentation practices.

1. **Adjective**: mandatory (no comparative/superlative - non-gradable)

   **Antonyms**: optional, voluntary, discretionary, elective, nonmandatory

   **Common Phrases**: 
   - mandatory requirement
   - mandatory training
   - mandatory compliance
   - mandatory reporting
   - legally mandatory
   
   **Root Analysis**: mandate (mand- [command] + -ate) + -ory (characterized by)
   
   **Etymology**: Latin mandatorius → English (16th century), meaning "of the nature of a command"
   
   **Examples [Casual]**: 
   - Attendance is mandatory for all employees.
   - It's mandatory to wear a helmet.
   
   **Examples [Formal]**: 
   - Disclosure is mandatory as per securities regulations.
   - Mandatory compliance audits occur annually.
