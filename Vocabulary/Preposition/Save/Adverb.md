1. **Adverb**: automatically (type: manner)
   **Meaning**: 
   - In a way that operates without human control or intervention; spontaneously
   - Mid-position or end-position in sentences
   
   **Synonyms**: mechanically, spontaneously, independently, self-actingly
   
   **Antonyms**: manually, deliberately, consciously, intentionally
   
   **When to Use**: Describing processes or actions that occur without active human involvement; technical contexts
   
   **When NOT to Use**: When emphasizing human agency or conscious decision-making; avoid in contexts requiring deliberation
   
   **Common Phrases**: 
   - automatically saved
   - automatically backed up
   - saved automatically
   
   **Root Analysis**: automatic (auto- "self" + -matic) + -ally (adverb suffix)
   
   **Etymology**: Greek automatos → automatic + -ally → automatically (brief)
   
   **Examples [Casual]**: 
   - The file saves automatically every few minutes.
   - My phone automatically backs up my photos.
   
   **Examples [Formal]**: 
   - Data is automatically preserved through redundant backup systems.
   - The application automatically saves all modifications to prevent data loss.
