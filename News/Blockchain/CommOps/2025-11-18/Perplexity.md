## Visual Assetsts---### Glossary**G1. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Total sales and marketing spend divided by new customers acquired; in blockchain/crypto, ranges $11.20-$18.20 for general platforms to $125 for crypto casinos. Analogous to "cost per user onboarded" in Web2; critical for LTV:CAC ratio (target 3:1 minimum).[36][31][25]

**G2. Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**: Percentage of revenue retained from existing customers including expansions minus churn/downgrades; blockchain platforms targeting 110-120% in 2025 (down from 130-140% peak). Measures upsell effectiveness—120% NRR means $100K cohort grows to $120K without new logos.[5]

**G3. Pipeline Velocity**: Daily revenue flow calculated as (Opportunities × Deal Size × Win Rate) / Sales Cycle Days; blockchain SaaS median $1,847/day. Faster velocity = more revenue with same headcount; 30-45 day cycles yield 38% velocity boost.[7]

**G4. Tokenomics**: Economic model governing token supply, distribution, utility, and incentive structures in blockchain ecosystems. Drives loyalty program design—e.g., staking rewards, governance rights, scarcity mechanisms—directly impacting customer engagement and retention.[18][10][11]

**G5. Churn Rate**: Percentage of customers canceling within a period; digital-first blockchain platforms achieve 10.8% vs. 17.6% traditional. Inverse of retention rate (89.2%); influenced by payment flexibility, UX, and localization.[2][30]
