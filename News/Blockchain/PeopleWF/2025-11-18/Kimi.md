## Glossary

**MiCA (Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation)**: EU framework regulating crypto asset issuance and trading. *Analogy*: Like GDPR but for crypto—non-compliance means massive fines and market access loss.

**Bitcoin DeFi**: Decentralized finance applications built on Bitcoin Layer-2 solutions. *Analogy*: Turning digital gold (Bitcoin) into a programmable financial system.

**Token Equity**: Compensation component granting company tokens vs. stock options. *Analogy*: Startup equity, but value fluctuates with crypto market cycles and protocol usage.

**AI-Web3 Convergence**: Integration of AI models with blockchain infrastructure for decentralized data oracles, automated smart contracts, and tokenized AI assets. *Analogy*: Combining the brain (AI) with a tamper-proof ledger (blockchain) to create self-executing intelligent systems.

**AI Audit**: Automated performance evaluation using AI analysis of employee digital output and tool usage. *Analogy*: A fitness tracker for work productivity—controversial and requires careful calibration.
