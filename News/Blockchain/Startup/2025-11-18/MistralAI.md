### Glossary (G#)

- **GENIUS Act**: U.S. federal framework for stablecoin issuers, providing regulatory clarity and fostering innovation in blockchain technology and digital assets.
- **MiCA**: European Union’s Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation, distinguishing between e-money tokens (EMT) and asset-referenced tokens (ART), ensuring stablecoins are backed by high-quality reserve assets.
- **CARF**: OECD’s Crypto-Asset Reporting Framework, ensuring transparency and compliance in the crypto market.
- **Stablecoins**: Digital tokens designed to maintain a stable value relative to a fixed amount of monetary value, backed by high-quality reserve assets.
- **DeFi**: Decentralized Finance, a financial system built on blockchain technology operating without centralized intermediaries.
- **NFTs**: Non-Fungible Tokens, unique digital assets representing ownership of specific items or content.
- **Smart Contracts**: Self-executing contracts with terms written into code, running on blockchain networks.
- **Blockchain Interoperability**: Ability of different blockchain networks to communicate and interact, enabling seamless transfer of data and assets.
