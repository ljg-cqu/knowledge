# MPC Wallet Engineer - Cloze Cards

1. Q: ___ stands for Multi-Party Computation, a cryptographic protocol enabling distributed key management.
   A: MPC

1. Q: The ___ protocol offers threshold ECDSA signing in 4 rounds, fixing GG20's security vulnerabilities.
   A: CGGMP21

1. Q: GG20 (Gennaro & Goldfeder 2020) implements threshold ECDSA in ___ rounds but has a known security vulnerability.
   A: 5 rounds

1. Q: ___ is a Flexible Round-Optimized Schnorr Threshold signature protocol that requires only 2 rounds.
   A: FROST

1. Q: List the 6 categories in the STRIDE threat modeling framework.
   A: Spoofing, Tampering, Repudiation, Information Disclosure, Denial of Service, Elevation of Privilege

1. Q: ERC-4337 is the Ethereum standard for ___.
   A: Account Abstraction

1. Q: The elliptic curve ___ is used by Bitcoin and Ethereum for ECDSA signatures.
   A: secp256k1

1. Q: Solana uses the ___ elliptic curve for EdDSA signatures.
   A: ed25519 (or Ed25519)

1. Q: ___ stands for Hardware Security Module, providing tamper-resistant key storage.
   A: HSM

1. Q: In MPC threshold signatures, a ___-of-___ scheme means 3 parties are needed out of 5 to sign.
   A: 3-of-5 (or threshold)

1. Q: WSJF prioritization framework calculates: (Business Value + Time Criticality + Risk Reduction) / ___.
   A: Job Size (or Effort)

1. Q: The target P95 mobile signing latency for competitive UX should be under ___ milliseconds.
   A: 800ms (or 1000ms, context-dependent)

1. Q: GG20 has a security vulnerability due to missing ___ in MtA share conversion.
   A: zero-knowledge range proofs

1. Q: ___ is a Deterministic Random Bit Generator standard for secure random number generation.
   A: DRBG (or NIST SP 800-90A)

1. Q: List the 4 DORA metrics for DevOps assessment.
   A: Deployment frequency, Lead time for changes, Change failure rate, Mean time to recovery (MTTR)

1. Q: BIP-174 defines the ___ standard for Bitcoin multi-party signing coordination.
   A: PSBT (Partially Signed Bitcoin Transaction)

1. Q: TEE stands for ___, a secure area of a processor for isolated execution.
   A: Trusted Execution Environment

1. Q: The ___ design pattern allows interface compatibility by wrapping incompatible interfaces.
   A: Adapter Pattern

1. Q: In Team Topologies, the three interaction modes are: Collaboration, ___, and Facilitation.
   A: X-as-a-Service

1. Q: SOC 2 Type II requires key rotation every ___ days for compliance.
   A: 90 days

1. Q: FIPS 140-2 Level ___ provides the highest tamper-resistance for HSMs.
   A: 3 (or 4)

1. Q: RFC 6979 specifies ___ usage of DSA and ECDSA to prevent nonce reuse attacks.
   A: Deterministic

1. Q: The formula for RICE prioritization is: (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / ___.
   A: Effort

1. Q: List the 3 layers in a modular multi-chain MPC architecture.
   A: Chain Abstraction Layer, Signature Scheme Adapter, RPC Client

1. Q: Pre-signing in CGGMP21 reduces online signing from 4 rounds to ___ round(s).
   A: 1

1. Q: In GDPR Article 33, data breach notification must be made within ___ hours.
   A: 72

1. Q: The CVSS scoring system ranges from ___ (least severe) to ___ (most severe).
   A: 0 to 10

1. Q: List the 5 phases of the NIST Cybersecurity Framework.
   A: Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover

1. Q: MPC wallet security should target key compromise probability < ___ % annually.
   A: 0.0001% (or 0.001%)

1. Q: EIP-712 defines typed structured data ___ and signing for Ethereum.
   A: hashing

1. Q: BIP-340 specifies ___ signatures for secp256k1 used in Bitcoin Taproot.
   A: Schnorr

1. Q: Paillier homomorphic encryption is used in threshold ___ protocols.
   A: ECDSA

1. Q: ADR stands for ___, used to document design decisions.
   A: Architecture Decision Record

1. Q: The target success rate for MPC transaction signing should be > ___%.
   A: 99.9% (or 99.5%)

1. Q: In a 2-of-3 threshold scheme, ___ key-shares are needed to reconstruct the private key.
   A: 2

1. Q: RACI matrix defines 4 roles: Responsible, Accountable, ___, and Informed.
   A: Consulted

1. Q: The recommended technical debt ratio should be < ___% of total engineering capacity.
   A: 30% (or 20%)

1. Q: SLA stands for Service Level ___.
   A: Agreement

1. Q: SLO stands for Service Level ___.
   A: Objective

1. Q: P99 latency measures the ___th percentile of response times.
   A: 99th

1. Q: NPS scores > ___ are considered excellent for customer satisfaction.
   A: 50

1. Q: The SOLID principles include Single Responsibility, Open-Closed, Liskov Substitution, Interface Segregation, and ___.
   A: Dependency Inversion

1. Q: MPC signing precomputation can reduce latency by approximately ___%.
   A: 60% (context-dependent)

1. Q: WebAssembly (WASM) provides ___ bytecode for portable cryptography across platforms.
   A: portable (or cross-platform)

1. Q: Mutual ___ authentication ensures both client and server verify each other's identity.
   A: TLS

1. Q: The MoSCoW prioritization method categorizes features as: Must-have, Should-have, Could-have, and ___.
   A: Won't-have

1. Q: Audit log completeness should be > ___% for SOC 2 compliance.
   A: 99.99%

1. Q: Chain RPC handlers should implement methods: getBalance, getNonce, broadcastTx, and ___.
   A: getTxStatus

1. Q: The recommended integration time for new blockchain support should be < ___ weeks.
   A: 2-6 weeks (context-dependent)

1. Q: TVL stands for Total Value ___ in blockchain ecosystems.
   A: Locked

1. Q: ARR stands for Annual Recurring ___ in business metrics.
   A: Revenue
