# LLM Multimedia: Essential Only

**Use ONLY if** blocks decision OR >40% gain OR WCAG required OR ≥2 roles. **75% less, 100% capability.**

## 6 Guidelines

**1. Format** [↓40-50%]: Type/format/purpose/limits | ❌ "Show X" ✅ "Mermaid sequence (SVG, <80): CI/CD→K8s+alt"
**2. Generate/Link** [↓50-70%]: Mermaid/ASCII or official only | ❌ "Show AWS" ✅ "Mermaid VPC/ALB/ECS, NO images"
**3. Source** [↓60-80%]: Official, verify | ❌ "Link video" ✅ "k8s.io/CNCF or text"
**4. WCAG 2.1 AA** [↑30-40%]: Alt/captions/text | ❌ "Add diagram" ✅ "Mermaid+text: parts/relations/decisions"
**5. Essential** [↓40-60%]: Decision-critical | ❌ "Images all" ✅ "Diagram: complex/≥3 parts/aids decision"
**6. Structure** [↑30-40%]: Context/meta/alt/key | ❌ Bare ✅ "Why→Media→Meta→Alt→Takeaway"

Include: Mermaid/ASCII, verified links, text alternatives, WCAG 2.1 AA, decision-critical only.
